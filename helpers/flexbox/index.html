Align items

Align items to top of flex container

Also align items available in responsive at all breakpoints sm, md, lg, xl

Class syntax: .as-items-{breakpoint}-{value}

  • .as-items-top
  • .as-items-bottom
  • .as-items-center
  • .as-items-{breakpoint}-top
  • .as-items-{breakpoint}-bottom
  • .as-items-{breakpoint}-center
